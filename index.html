About

Burnt™ was founded in the fall of 2022 by five life-long friends with a passion for fitness. Burnt is the premiere workout routine building website that will help reach your fitness goals no matter where you are in your fitness journey.

We offer 3 programs to help you reach your specific goals:

1. Tone Up–tighten up the muscles and give them shape.
2. Get Built–increase muscle mass and make your muscles bigger.
3. Get Sweaty–lose weight and burn fat.

After you pick your program, we’ll build your fitness routine for you. You can complete your workout routine from the comfort of your home, or at the gym near you. Are you ready to get BURNT?
